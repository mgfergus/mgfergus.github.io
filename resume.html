My Language Learning Journey

The Beginning

It started a year and a half ago. It was during my freshman year in college, and I already finished my first semester. I was talking with my cousin over the phone, and he told me about his aspirations to go to Japan and start a business there. I joked with him saying that he should learn the language if he wanted to do that. He then told me that we'll both learn the language together with the thought of traveling to Japan one day. This then sparked an idea in my head. I should be capable of learning a language, how hard could it be? In retrospect, I realize how naive I was. So there began my language journey. It started with Japanese and now I have taken an interest in Mandarin. I am no where near as good as I would like to be, however I'm hoping with time, that will change.

Languages I am Studying

  • Japanese
  • Mandarin

What I Have Learned

Staying motivated is hard when learning a language. That is why one must make the process fun, as one can easily burn themselves out in the long run.

"The goal has never been to always succeed. The goal is to be allowed to keep initiating." - Seth Goden

Figuring out what study method best fits me helped me out a lot. While a lot of effort is involved when studying a language, the process becomes a lot smoother when you figure out the right way for you.

"Don't study hard. Study smart" - Amy Lucas
