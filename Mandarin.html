Mandarin

Overview

Mandarin is a difficult language for English speakers due to the fact that it completely different than English. They are only slightly similar in their grammar structure, however, other than that, they are completely different. There is Simplified and Traditional Chinese. I studied Simplified Chinese as that is used in mainland China. The difference between the two is that Simplified Chinese has characters that have been simplified to make it easier to read and write. Mandarin uses a script called hànzì. There are over 50,000 characters, however one only needs to know 2,000 characters to be considered literate. The pronunciation of the characters are taught through a system called Pinyin that uses the English alphabet to spell out the word.
